## 3. Precursor Affiliation
*   **Visual Engine Optimisation (VEO):** An independent framework developed and published by EnBra Group LLC.

VEO addresses machine-level visual interpretability through the optimisation of visual clarity, structural composition, semantic recognition, UI/UX machine readability, and multimodal alignment.

Within the SIO architecture, VEO operates as an upstream optimisation discipline supplying machine-vision signals to the broader perception layer.

VEO remains a distinct framework and is referenced within SIO solely as a foundational EnBra Group optimisation discipline.
